Game Rules

1. Objective: Defeat your opponent by reducing their Health Points (HP) to zero.

2. Accounts & Setup: An account is required. Each match is 1v1. Both players start with [X] HP and a hand of [N] cards. The first turn is random.

3. Your Turn: You have a time limit (e.g., 30 seconds). Draw cards to maintain [N] cards in hand. Play character cards from your hand by paying their Cost. Use active cards to Attack your opponent's cards or their HP directly.

4. Cards: Each card displays the character's art, name, Attack (ATK), Defense (DEF), and Cost. Stronger cards cost more.

5. Winning & Losing: You win if your opponent's HP reaches 0. You lose if your HP reaches 0.

6. Fair Play:

  • No offensive language, harassment, or discrimination.
  • Do not use cheats, hacks, or exploits. Play honestly.
  • You are responsible for your account security; do not share login details.
  • Avoid spamming chat or game actions.
  • Report bugs, exploits, or rule violations.
